About SchemeFinder

Our Vision

At SchemeFinder, our vision is to bridge the gap between government welfare programs and the citizens who need them most. We believe that easy access to information is the first step towards empowerment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of discovering and understanding government schemes. Through a user-friendly platform, we aim to provide personalized eligibility checks, comprehensive scheme details, and multilingual support to ensure no one is left behind.

Our Team

We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about leveraging technology for social good. Our experts in public policy, software development, and user experience work tirelessly to make government schemes accessible to everyone.
